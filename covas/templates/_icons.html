{# Themable icon sprite (issue #214). ONE inline SVG of defs, {% include %}'d once per page near the top of ; icons are referenced anywhere on that page via Icon-only buttons keep a title/aria-label for #184. Glyphs are Lucide (https://lucide.dev, ISC-licensed) paths, embedded inline — no font, no runtime dependency, no network (matters for the packaged offline app + the stdlib-first rule). They draw with `stroke:currentColor`, so every icon inherits the theme tokens (--text/--accent/--muted) and recolours across the dark / light / elite themes for free — unlike the full-colour emoji they replace. `ic-reset` (rotate-ccw) is deliberately distinct from `ic-refresh` (refresh-cw). #}

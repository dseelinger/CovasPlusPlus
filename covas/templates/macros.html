Author a macro

A macro can only use actions in your [keybinds].allowlist — that's what keeps it safe (it can't do anything you haven't already enabled). Add a trigger to auto-run it on a game event, or leave it blank and say "run name". Consequential macros ask for a spoken confirm first. You can also author entirely by voice: "create a macro called…".
